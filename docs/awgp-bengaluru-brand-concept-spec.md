# AWGP Bengaluru — Brand Concept Spec

**Version 1.0**
*All World Gayatri Pariwar · Bengaluru Chapter*

हम बदलेंगे, युग बदलेगा | हम सुधरेंगे, युग सुधरेगा | — *Transform the self, and the era follows.*

---

## 1. The core idea

**Ancient light, modern life.**

AWGP's founding truth is that when a person transforms within, the era transforms around them (*Hum badlenge, yug badlega. Hum sudhrenge, yug sudhrega*). The entire brand expresses this single idea through one symbol: **light** — the flame of the Gayatri tradition, raised by the hands of a community, made calm and contemporary for the people of Bengaluru.

Light is the bridge that lets the brand be reverent and modern at once. It is ancient (yagna fire, the diya), it is scientific (energy, dawn, the *savitur* of the Gayatri Mantra), and it is contemporary (clean, minimal, glowing). One symbol, three registers — which is why it can serve temple-going families and 28-year-old engineers from the same system.

**The job of this brand:** make fifty different deliverables — website, deck, flyer, reel, merch — feel like one voice.

| Element | Definition |

| **Promise** | Ancient wisdom you can actually live by — in a city that never slows down. |

| **English taglines** | Awaken the light within. · Self transformed, world transformed. · Ancient wisdom, living daily. |

### Audience

Bengaluru's full spectrum — young professionals seeking focus and meaning, families continuing a practice, and lifelong members. **The brand leads young and modern, then reveals its depth.** It never opens with heaviness.

### The tone strategy (how to be "everything at once")

The brief asks for calm + vibrant + traditional + premium + minimal + modern simultaneously. No single element can carry all of those — the **system** carries them through a hierarchy of layers:

1. **Base layer — calm & premium.** Warm cream space, restrained maroon, refined serif. This is most of every layout.
2. **Accent layer — vibrant & energetic.** Gold and saffron used sparingly, as "light": glows, a single sunburst, a gold divider.
3. **Structure layer — modern & minimal.** Generous spacing, clean grid, sans-serif body.
4. **Symbol & language layer — traditional & reverent.** Tradition lives in the torch mark, the sunburst, and the Hindi anchor line — **not** in clutter.

> **The one rule that keeps it consistent:** base calm, accent precious, tradition in the symbols and the anchor line. When a layout feels off — add cream and remove one accent.

## 3. Color

Deep Maroon stays primary; it carries devotion and authority. Temple Gold is the "light" — precious, used at roughly one-tenth of any layout so it always feels special. Warm Cream is the breathing space that makes everything premium.

**The single rule that keeps the brand modern:** maroon should rarely appear without cream around it and a thread of gold within it.

| Name | Hex | Role |
|---|---|---|
| Deep Maroon | `#8B1E1E` | Primary. Headers, anchors, brand blocks. |
| Maroon Deep | `#6E1414` | Shadows, gradient depth. |
| Temple Gold | `#C9A227` | The light. Accents, CTAs, dividers (~10%). |
| Dawn Saffron | `#E8943B` | Energy accent for youth & social. |
| Warm Cream | `#F7F1E5` | Primary background. The calm. |
| Ink | `#2B221E` | Body text. Warm near-black — never pure `#000`. |
| Sage | `#5E7367` | Single cool tone for charts & illustration depth. |
| Line | `#E4D9C4` | Hairlines, borders, quiet structure. |

### Usage balance

Treat every layout as roughly **60% cream · 25% maroon · 10% ink text · 5% gold.** Saffron borrows from gold's share on youth-facing pieces.

### Contrast rules (accessibility)

- Gold (`#C9A227`) on cream **fails small-text contrast** — use it for large display, lines, and shapes, never body copy.
- Text on maroon → use cream (`#F7F1E5`).
- Text on cream → use ink (`#2B221E`).

## 4. Typography

The type system *is* the brand idea: a refined serif for the ancient and premium, a clean sans for the modern and clear, and an elegant Devanagari so bilingual content never falls back to system fonts.

| Role | Typeface | Use | Alternatives |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Display** | Fraunces | Headlines, hero statements, pull quotes, wordmark. Weights 400–600, used with restraint. | Marcellus, Cormorant |
| **Body & UI** | Plus Jakarta Sans | All body, navigation, buttons, labels, captions, website UI. Weights 400/500/600/700. | Inter |
| **Devanagari** | Tiro Devanagari Hindi | All Hindi/Sanskrit — mantras, anchor line, bilingual captions. | Mukta |

### Type scale

| Level | Size |
|---|---|
| Display / H1 | 54–84px |
| Section / H2 | 32–46px |
| Subhead / H3 | 22–26px |
| Lead paragraph | 18–20px / 1.6 |
| Body | 16px / 1.6 |
| Caption / label | 12px · uppercase · tracked (.18em) · weight 700 |

The serif-display + sans-body pairing is itself the "ancient + modern" idea expressed in type.

## 5. Logo

### Current state

The existing mark — a **torch raised by a fist rising from the community**, with a radiant halo — is the brand's real equity. The composition is genuinely strong and on-concept: light raised up, supported by and lifting the collective. **Keep it.**

What holds it back today: the illustration is painterly and photo-realistic while the wordmark is flat and clean — two different design languages glued together. The crowd has too many tiny figures that turn to mud at small sizes; the realistic rendering can never become a single flat color (needed for watermarks, embroidery, stamps); and the whole thing only works on a black background, which fights the cream-based premium direction.

### Direction: a logo family, not one lockup

1. **Simplify the illustration into a clean vector** — same composition (fist + torch + halo + a *suggested* crowd as a simple arc of stylized figures, not 40 detailed ones). Flatten shading to 3–4 tones max so it works in full color, single maroon, and single gold.
2. **Build a tiered system:**
   - *Primary lockup* — illustration + "AWGP / All World Gayatri Pariwar / Bengaluru" (current layout, refined).
   - *Horizontal lockup* — simplified mark + "AWGP Bengaluru" beside it, for site headers.
   - *Icon* — torch-in-fist with halo, no crowd, for favicon / avatar / app.
3. **Make it background-agnostic** — design a version that works on cream, not only black. The halo glow can sit on cream beautifully if the flame keeps contrast.
4. **Fix the type relationship** — set "AWGP" in the display serif (Fraunces / Marcellus) so wordmark and illustration feel intentional together; tighten "BENGALURU" so it reads as part of the system, not an afterthought.

### Color treatments

- **Full colour** on cream (primary).
- **Single gold** reversed on maroon.
- **Single maroon** on cream (stamp / one-colour).

### Clear space & minimum size

- Clear space on all sides equal to the **height of the flame** — nothing intrudes.
- Minimum sizes: icon no smaller than **24px** (digital) / **10mm** (print); primary lockup no smaller than **120px** wide. Below that, switch to the icon-only tier.

### Do

- Use the simplified vector versions for anything below poster size.
- Reverse to single gold on maroon, single maroon on cream.
- Keep the flame, fist, and community together — they're one idea.
- Give it room. Clear space protects the mark.

### Don't

- Don't use the painterly version at small sizes — it turns to mud.
- Don't place full-colour art on busy photos without a panel behind it.
- Don't recolour the flame to off-brand hues or add drop shadows / bevels.
- Don't stretch, rotate, or rearrange the lockup.

## 6. Signature motif — the radiant mark

One ownable device appears across everything and ties the system together: **light emanating from a single point.**

It is the halo behind the flame, a corner flourish on a flyer, a section divider on the site, a loading animation, and the opening sting of every video — rays expanding from a point of light. Repeating this one element is what makes every deliverable feel like the same hand made it.

**Forms of the motif:**

- **Sunburst** — radiating gold lines from a maroon center. The hero device.
- **Concentric halo** — nested circles (line / gold / maroon). Quiet structural version.
- **Gold divider** — a thin gold rule with a center diamond (`◆`). For section breaks and lockups.
- **Dawn gradient** — saffron-to-gold bar. Energy accent for youth and social pieces.

## 7. Voice & tone

Warm, clear, never preachy. We speak *with* people, not down at them. Sanskrit and Hindi terms are welcome but always explained in plain language. For younger audiences, lead with relevance — focus, calm, purpose, community — and let the spiritual depth follow.

| We are | We are not |
|---|---|
| Warm | Preachy |
| Clear | Cryptic |
| Grounded | Salesy |
| Welcoming | Heavy |
| Uplifting | Exclusive |
| Bilingual | Cluttered |

**In practice:**

> "Yagna — a sacred fire ritual for collective wellbeing — every Sunday at 7am. Come as you are."

The term is introduced, glossed in plain words, made inviting. The Hindi anchor line carries the *feeling*; the English carries the *meaning*.

## 8. Application — one system, every surface

The same palette, the radiant motif, and the serif-plus-sans pairing carry across the whole project. Each surface emphasizes a different layer of the brand.

| Surface | Direction |
|---|---|
| **Website** | Reskin the Gita theme structure (hero, four pillars(sadhna, swadhyay, sanyam, seva), path of life - (sanskars, events, activities), upcoming events, chetna kendra, contact, join us CTA, donation, blogs) into cream-first layouts with maroon blocks and gold accents. More breathing room than the original. Palette is ours, not the theme's. |
| **Pitch deck** | Opens on the sunburst and the anchor line. Serif headlines, generous cream space, one idea per slide, gold for emphasis only. |
| **Social posts** | Saffron-forward energy on cream or maroon. Icon as a quiet watermark. Serif headline + one clear message + the anchor line. |
| **Flyers** | Serif headline, one strong image, a gold divider, clear event details in sans. Cream base keeps it premium. |
| **Video** | Open and close on the expanding-light sting. Lower-thirds in sans; titles in serif; a gold rule under each. |
| **Print & merch** | Single-colour icon for stamps, totes, embroidery. Maroon on cream, or gold foil for premium pieces. |

### Social template patterns

- **Maroon · energy accent:** maroon-deep gradient with a saffron glow top-center; serif headline; Hindi anchor line in soft gold; event details in sans. *(e.g. "Begin your week in stillness." — Sundays · 7:00 AM)*
- **Cream · calm & premium:** cream background, maroon serif pull-quote, ink caption. *(e.g. "Transform the self, and the era follows.")*
